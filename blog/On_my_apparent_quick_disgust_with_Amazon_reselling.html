On my apparent quick disgust with Amazon reselling

Created on: 2023-01-16 16:53:40

Modified on: 2023-01-16 16:54:19

Scope: No scope provided.

Table of Contents

I sit here at my keyboard on January 11th, 2023. I just moved to Dallas TX about 4 months ago, and have started a job as an engineer.

I just got home from a day at the office, cleaned up a bit, ate some food, and sat down at my desk to resume my career as an Amazon reseller.

At first I had a lot of fun, I was poking through my old spreadsheets, looking through my profit calculations, looking at what products were in my product research.

I made it 16 minutes into the 45 minutes I allotted for this task before I realized this new phase of my life has caused me to have a slight disgust for the profession.

There’s no uniqueness to the process. You are a link in the chain we call consumerism. Doing nothing but being a slight leech in the system. 

I want to exercise my ability to create and layer a business on top of that, not just the business part.
